    WHY ETO ?

    At ETO, Great interior design is our business and our passion.We believe that any place becomes a home when it's personalized — your space should show off your tastes and personality, work with your lifestyle, help you with your work and maybe make your guests a little jealous. We know that creating an interior that does all of these things isn't easy, and we're here to help.

    Our services

    Our talented designers bring more than 20 years of experience to the table, couch, coffee table, or whichever furniture piece we're sitting near. From discovering your personal style to knocking down that wall that's been preventing the open-concept living area you've been lusting after, we'll make sure that your space isn't just aesthetically pleasing, but also a reflection of you.

    We offer the following services:

    • On-site consultations
    • Project survey & analysis
    • Space planning & furniture arrangement
    • Design concepts
    • Finishes & furnishings
    • Custom designs
    • Purchasing, delivery, & installation
    • Project coordination & management
